Cafe Description
The Supper Club is the hipster communities' response to Hither Green's growing gentrification. Come to enjoy our freshly made coffee and
homemade vegan snacks or just occupy one of our very few tables to answer emails all morning - our WiFi is free and super fast! If you
don't have time to stop in we sell a range organic pastas and ridiculous super foods.
We hope to see you soon!
